2. How We Use Information

  • To provide App features (logging, aggregation, analysis, display)
  • To provide AI coach features (menu generation, meal photo analysis, chat, weekly reports)
  • To provide social features (follows, rankings, etc.)
  • To provide subscriptions/in-app purchases and manage usage limits
  • For troubleshooting, quality improvement, and responding to inquiries

3. Handling of HealthKit Data

  • Health data obtained from HealthKit is used to assist, display, and analyze your records within the App.
  • For the Sleep Tracking feature (Coach plan), nightly computed summaries (sleep duration, time in each stage, sleep score, average/lowest heart rate, average HRV, etc.) are stored on our server (Supabase) to sync across your devices. The raw HealthKit samples themselves are not stored.
  • We do not use information obtained from HealthKit for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • We do not store information obtained from HealthKit in iCloud.
  • We do not sell or disclose information obtained from HealthKit to third parties without your consent.
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in the Apple Health settings.

5. Information Made Public via Social Features

Your username, profile, and certain aggregated values (rankings, streak days, etc.) may be shown to other users through the App's social features. Please do not enter information you do not wish to make public.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • You can delete your account from within the App; related data on our servers will be deleted within a reasonable period.
  • Data stored on your device (local records) is removed when you delete the App.
  • Information subject to a legal retention obligation may be retained for the required period.

7. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards, such as encryption in transit and access controls, to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of collected information.

8. Minors

Because the App includes social features, the intended age is 17 and over. If a minor uses the App, please do so with the consent of a parent or guardian.
